Burgers

How can you bet a juicy smoked flavored Hamburger off the grill, with a side of crispy fries to occompany it with

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Fries

Steps

  1. Season your ground beef to your liking and then roll into the shape a baseball or larger, and press down into patties.
  2. Heat a skillet to medium heat. Once warm place the patties in the skill until browned evenly
  3. While the meat is cooking, you can chop up the lettuce, tomatoes, and onions. Set these to the side after
  4. Once the patties are done cooking, and cooled off. The only thing left to do is make your burger and eat
  5. OHHHH, we didn't make the fries! Well you can just read the insturctions on the bag and throw them in an air fryer
